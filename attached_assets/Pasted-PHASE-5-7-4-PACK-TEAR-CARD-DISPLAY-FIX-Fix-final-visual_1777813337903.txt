PHASE 5.7.4 — PACK TEAR + CARD DISPLAY FIX

Fix final visual bugs in the pack opening system.

Observed issues:

• duplicated pack art slices when touching seam  
• seam guide still visible after tear  
• pack remains behind cards after opening  
• card borders cropped  
• tear clip-path not activating  

Do NOT modify:

RNG engine  
cardRevealAnimator  
packSimulation  
rarity logic  

Only modify pack-opening UI.

---

SECTION 1 — FIX PACK TOP DUPLICATION

Do NOT redraw pack art as background.

Use clipped image instead.

HTML structure:

<div class="booster-pack">
  <div class="pack-top">
     <img src="pack-art.png">
  </div>

  <div class="pack-bottom">
     <img src="pack-art.png">
  </div>
</div>

CSS:

.pack-top{

position:absolute;
top:0;
left:0;

width:100%;
height:22%;

overflow:hidden;

}

.pack-bottom{

position:absolute;
top:22%;
left:0;

width:100%;
height:78%;

overflow:hidden;

}

.pack-bottom img{

position:absolute;
top:-22%;

width:100%;
height:auto;

}

This ensures the top and bottom sections show the SAME image without repetition.

---

SECTION 2 — REMOVE SEAM AFTER TEAR

When tear completes:

JS:

seamLine.remove()

OR

document.body.classList.add("pack-opened")

CSS:

.pack-opened .pack-seam{
display:none;
}

---

SECTION 3 — ACTIVATE TEAR CLIP

Ensure the class is applied.

JS:

boosterPack.classList.add("torn")

CSS:

.booster-pack.torn{

clip-path: polygon(
0% 12%,
100% 12%,
100% 100%,
0% 100%
);

}

This removes the top portion visually.

---

SECTION 4 — REMOVE PACK AFTER CARDS EMERGE

After cards slide up:

JS:

boosterPack.remove()

This prevents the wrapper from being visible behind cards.

---

SECTION 5 — FIX CARD CROPPING

.card-back img{

width:100%;
height:100%;

object-fit:contain;

border-radius:10px;

}

Remove any:

overflow:hidden
object-fit:cover

from the card container.

---

SECTION 6 — EXPECTED RESULT

User opens pack:

Pack appears.

User drags across seam.

Top foil tears away.

Seam disappears.

Cards slide upward.

Pack wrapper disappears.

Full card borders visible.

Reveal system proceeds normally.

END PATCH
